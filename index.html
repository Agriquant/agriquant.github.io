Introducing Agriquant

September 18, 2025

Agriquant is a research-driven startup developing the first algorithmic risk and decision platform for agriculture in Uruguay and LATAM.

Uruguay's agri-food sector generates billions in export value each year (beef, dairy, rice alone exceeded US$3.5B in 2023). Yet production margins remain exposed to unhedged volatility. The 2022–23 drought brought rainfall ~47% below normal, causing ~US$1.8B in direct losses.

Case Study: The Invisible Crash
Nov 2022 Analysis
Surface Signal (Visual) Statistical Reality (Risk)
The Alpha Gap: On the left, visual inspection shows vegetation presence (green). On the right, our Z-Score engine reveals a -2.5 Sigma deviation (red) in the central productive core. This statistical anomaly signaled the livestock liquidation event 3 weeks before market consensus.

Input prices and currencies move sharply, affecting sowing, feeding, logistics, and hedging decisions. Our mission is to make risk intelligence for agriculture systematic and democratized, establishing a transparent standard for data-conscious decision-making.
