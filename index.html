Introducing Agriquant

September 18, 2025

Agriquant is a research-driven startup developing the first algorithmic risk and decision platform for agriculture in Uruguay and LATAM.

Uruguay's agri-food sector generates billions in export value each year (beef, dairy, rice alone exceeded US$3.5B in 2023). Yet production and margins are highly exposed to weather, input-cost, and FX shocks. The 2022–23 drought brought rainfall ~47% below normal and caused ~US$1.8B in direct losses. Input prices and currencies also move sharply, affecting sowing, feeding, logistics and hedging decisions.

Quantification of Stress. Experts familiar with Uruguay’s seasonal NDVI patterns may recognize early stress in the natural-color view (Right). However, only the Standardized Anomaly (Left) quantifies the deviation (≈−2.5σ) relative to the historical baseline, revealing the full severity and confirming that optical greenness alone is a lagging indicator during onset drought phases.

Our mission is to make risk intelligence for agriculture systematic and democratized, and to establish a transparent standard in the industry for data-conscious decision-making.
