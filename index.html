Introducing Agriquant

September 18, 2025

Agriquant is a research-driven startup developing the first algorithmic risk and decision platform for agriculture in Uruguay and LATAM.

Uruguay's agri-food sector generates billions in export value each year (beef, dairy, rice alone exceeded US$3.5B in 2023). Our mission is to make risk intelligence for agriculture systematic and democratized, and to establish a transparent standard in the industry for data-conscious decision-making.
